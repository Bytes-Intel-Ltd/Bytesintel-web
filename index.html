Our Process

We make awesome
Products with strategy...

01

Idea.

Going through our client’s design document to better understand the scope of the project. This will enable us come up functional and business requirements/needs of the project.

02

Planning.

After an in depth understanding of the project’s scope, we’ll define the project plan into functional requirements for the Design and Developmental stages.

03

Design.

Having concluded our planning stage, our next step is to convert our learnings into an intuitive visual representation of the end product.

04

Development.

This stage caters for implementing the functionalities agreed upon in the Discovery and Planning stages. After completion of the unit functionality, quality testing and verification follows by the internal team.

05

Testing.

Reviewing the functionalities of the products with the client against their specified rules in a testing environment. Reporting and resolving any indeficiency.

06

Launch.

After the total revision of the entire system and defects detected had been resolved. The product is released to its intended consumer.
